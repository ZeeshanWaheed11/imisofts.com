Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our review service

Is this really safe for my Shopify account?

Absolutely. We've been doing this for 5+ years with zero account issues. Our reviewers are real, verified Shopify merchants with established stores and purchase histories. They genuinely install and test your app. We follow every Shopify guideline to the letter. Your developer account is 100% safe.

When will I see results?

First reviews appear within 72 hours. Install rates typically increase 50-100% within the first week as reviews accumulate. By day 30, most clients see 200-400% growth in installs. The Shopify algorithm also boosts your visibility as your rating improves, creating a compound effect.

Who actually writes these reviews?

Our network includes 500+ verified Shopify merchants across fashion, electronics, home goods, beauty, and 20+ other verticals. These are real store owners running real businesses. They test apps regularly as part of their business operations. Each reviewer has an established Shopify account with verifiable history.

Can I influence what the reviews say?

You can highlight key features and benefits you want emphasized. We ensure reviews naturally incorporate important keywords for SEO. However, reviews are written independently based on actual testing experience. This authenticity is what makes them effective and keeps them compliant.

What if I get a negative review elsewhere?

Our Growth and Scale packages include crisis management. We help craft professional responses and can accelerate positive review publishing to maintain your rating. Having a buffer of positive reviews means one negative review becomes a small percentage rather than a devastating blow.

How do reviews look natural to Shopify?

Strategic timing is key. Reviews are published over weeks, not days. We vary review lengths, writing styles, and include authentic 4-star reviews alongside 5-stars. Reviews come from diverse geographic locations and merchant profiles. The pattern perfectly mimics organic accumulation.

What's your refund policy?

30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with review quality or don't see increased installs, we refund your first month completely. No questions asked. We also guarantee all reviews will be 4.5 stars or higher and fully compliant. Our 98% retention rate speaks for itself.

Can I pause or cancel anytime?

Yes, absolutely. No contracts, no minimums. Cancel or pause with one email. Most clients never cancel because the ROI is so strong - reviews typically generate 10-50x their cost in new revenue. You can also upgrade or downgrade plans anytime based on your growth needs.
