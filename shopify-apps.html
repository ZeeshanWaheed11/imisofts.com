Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our Shopify app development services

How much does custom Shopify app development cost? +

Custom Shopify app development ranges from $10,000 to $45,000 depending on complexity and features. This includes design, development, testing, and guaranteed Shopify App Store approval. We have built 500+ apps with an 87% install rate.

How long does it take to build a Shopify app? +

Most Shopify apps are built and approved within 4-6 weeks. We use Remix and Shopify's Polaris design system for rapid development. Our standard delivery is 35 days from kickoff to App Store listing.

Do you guarantee Shopify App Store approval? +

Yes, we guarantee App Store approval. We are Built for Shopify certified and follow all Shopify API best practices, Polaris design guidelines, and performance standards. If your app is not approved, we will rework it at no additional cost.

What types of Shopify apps do you build? +

We build conversion boosters, subscription management apps, inventory and operations tools, reviews and UGC platforms, search and merchandising solutions, and analytics and reporting dashboards. We use React, Node.js, Remix, and Shopify APIs.

Can you help with an existing Shopify app that needs improvement? +

Absolutely. We help optimize existing Shopify apps for performance, improve UX using Polaris, fix App Store compliance issues, add new features, and improve install and retention rates. We also offer our Shopify App Reviews service to boost your app visibility.
