# Cold Email Spam Trigger Words: 50+ Words to Avoid in 2026

One word. That's all it takes.

Use one spam trigger word, and your email gets flagged by filters. One phrase, and your deliverability tanks.

At imisofts, we've tested 10,000+ email variations. We know which words kill your campaign instantly.

Here are 50+ trigger words to avoid, plus what to say instead.

## The Spam Word Categories

Email filters focus on three categories:

1. **Sales-y words** ("opportunity," "solution," "increase")
2. **Urgency triggers** ("act now," "limited time," "urgent")
3. **Too-good-to-be-true claims** ("free," "100%," "guaranteed")

## 50+ Spam Trigger Words + Replacements

### Sales-y Words to Avoid

| Avoid | Say Instead |
|-------|-------------|
| Lead generation | Outbound prospecting |
| Opportunity | Idea / Angle / Thought |
| Marketing solution | Approach / Strategy |
| Sales opportunity | Conversation |
| Increase sales | Book more meetings |
| Boost revenue | Generate pipeline |
| Growth solution | Growth strategy |
| Revenue growth | More pipeline |
| Enterprise solution | Platform / Tool |
| Win-win | Mutual benefit |
| Unlock potential | Explore |

### Urgency Triggers to Avoid

| Avoid | Say Instead |
|-------|-------------|
| Act now | Let me know |
| Limited time | This month / This quarter |
| Urgent | Important / Worth exploring |
| ASAP | Soon |
| Hurry | Quick call |
| Don't delay | Consider |
| Final notice | One more thought |
| Last chance | Last email |
| Time-sensitive | [Specific timing reason] |
| Expires | Spots filling |

### Too-Good-to-Be-True Claims

| Avoid | Say Instead |
|-------|-------------|
| Free | No cost / Complimentary |
| 100% | 98% / Most |
| Guaranteed | Seen in [number] cases |
| Risk-free | Money-back / No risk |
| Exclusive | One-time / Limited spots |
| Special offer | Custom proposal |
| Best | Most effective |
| #1 | Ranked #1 / Top-rated |
| Award-winning | Recognized for [specific thing] |
| Proven | Tested with [number] teams |
| Works instantly | Results within [timeframe] |

## All-Caps Words (Never Use)

These are death in cold email:

- CHECK THIS OUT
- AMAZING OPPORTUNITY
- DON'T WAIT
- ACT NOW
- CLICK HERE
- MUST READ
- URGENT
- IMPORTANT
- FINAL OFFER
- SECRET

**Rule: Never use ALL CAPS in cold email, ever.**

FAQ_SCHEMA:
Q: What's the single most harmful spam trigger word?
A: "Free." It flags spam filters and screams low-quality pitch. Avoid entirely. If it's actually free, just don't mention it—let them discover it.

Q: Does one spam trigger word tank the entire email?
A: Not always. One word might reduce deliverability 5-10%. Multiple trigger words (3+) can tank you to 20-30% spam folder rate.

Q: Are there spam words specific to certain industries?
A: Yes. Healthcare/medical have: "cure," "miracle," "prescription." Finance have: "guaranteed returns," "can't lose." Use our industry-specific lists.

Q: How do I know if a word is a spam trigger?
A: Use Boomerang or Postmark's spam checker. But also use common sense: if it sounds like mass marketing, it probably is.

Q: Can I use capitalization to make words seem less spammy?
A: No. If anything, capitalization (I HAVE AN OPPORTUNITY) makes it worse. Stick to lowercase, conversational tone.
