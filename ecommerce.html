Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our e-commerce development services

How much does it cost to build a Shopify store with imisofts? +

Our Shopify store development packages range from $5,000 to $35,000 depending on complexity. This includes custom theme design, payment integration, shipping setup, and marketing automation. We have built 300+ stores processing $250M+ in gross merchandise value.

How long does it take to launch a Shopify store? +

We can launch a fully functional Shopify store in as little as 2 weeks. Our standard delivery timeline is 28 days from kickoff to go-live, including custom design, product setup, payment integration, and QA testing.

What conversion rate can I expect from my Shopify store? +

Our stores achieve a 4.2% average conversion rate, significantly above the industry average of 1.4%. We accomplish this through strategic UX design, optimized checkout flows, and conversion rate optimization techniques proven across 300+ builds.

Do you support dropshipping and COD payments? +

Yes. We offer complete dropshipping suite setup including supplier integration, automated order routing, and multi-warehouse fulfillment. We also support cash on delivery (COD) payment integration, which is essential for GCC and MENA markets.

Which marketing tools do you integrate with Shopify? +

We integrate Klaviyo, HubSpot, Facebook Shop, Instagram Shopping, Google Merchant Center, and TikTok Shop. We also set up email automation, abandoned cart recovery, and retargeting campaigns to maximize your store revenue.
