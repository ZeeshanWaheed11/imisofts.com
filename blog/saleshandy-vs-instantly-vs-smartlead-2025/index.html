Frequently Asked Questions

Which is cheaper: Saleshandy, Instantly, or Smartlead?

Saleshandy offers the best value starting at $25/month with unlimited email accounts. Instantly starts at $37/month, and Smartlead at $39/month. All include unlimited warmup, but Saleshandy includes more features at lower price points.

Which cold email tool has the best deliverability?

Based on our testing of 5M+ emails: Saleshandy achieved 96% inbox rate, Instantly 94%, and Smartlead 92%. All three offer excellent deliverability with proper setup, but Saleshandy performed consistently better across different industries.

Do all three platforms offer unlimited email accounts?

Yes, Saleshandy, Instantly, and Smartlead all offer unlimited email account connections on their plans, making them scalable for high-volume campaigns. However, there may be practical limits on sequences and concurrent sends.

Which tool is best for agencies managing multiple clients?

Saleshandy offers the best agency pricing with unlimited clients included in higher plans. Smartlead charges $29/client/month, and Instantly charges per user. For a 10-client agency, Saleshandy costs $139/month vs Smartlead's $329/month and Instantly's $970/month.
