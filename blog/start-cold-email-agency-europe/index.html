How to Start a Cold Email Agency in Europe: The Market Opportunity

Most agencies focus on the US market. This is a mistake. Europe is less competitive, highly fragmented by regulation, and underserved by specialized providers.

The numbers are stunning:

  • UK: Only 23-30 cold email agencies (vs. 500+ in US)
  • Germany: <10 agencies (strict compliance = less competition)
  • Netherlands: <5 agencies (concentrated opportunity)
  • Ireland: 15-20 agencies (easy to displace with focus)
  • Sweden/Finland: <3 agencies combined

This isn't small business. It's opportunity.

The 2026 European Cold Email Agency Model

Target Market: European B2B SaaS companies seeking customer acquisition through cold email.

Service Offering:

  • Campaign strategy and messaging
  • List building (5,000-25,000 qualified prospects)
  • Email template creation
  • Instantly/SmartLead setup and warmup
  • 30-60-90 day managed campaigns
  • Monthly reporting and optimization

Pricing Model:

  • Micro: €199-299/month (entry, done-for-you)
  • Standard: €599-899/month (managed campaigns, optimization)
  • Premium: €1,499-2,499/month (white-label, priority support)
  • Enterprise: €5,000+/month (custom, multi-market expansion)

Revenue Projection:

  • 10 customers at €600/month = €6K MRR (€72K ARR)
  • 30 customers = €18K MRR (€216K ARR)
  • 50 customers = €30K MRR (€360K ARR)
  • 100 customers = €60K MRR (€720K ARR)

Most successful European cold email agencies hit €500K-€2M ARR within 3 years.

Building Your European Agency: 6-Month Plan

Month 1: Niche Selection + List Building

Choose Your Primary Market:

  1. UK (largest opportunity, highest volume, PECR compliance)
  2. Ireland (concentrated, underserved, same as UK regulatory)
  3. Germany (highest contract value, strict compliance = less competition)
  4. Netherlands (SaaS-dense, single opt-in permissive)

Build Master List of 26,500+ European Prospects:

  • Scrape Apollo: 10,000-15,000 contacts (UK, Ireland, Netherlands, Germany, Sweden)
  • LinkedIn: 3,000-5,000 high-value contacts (founders, VPs)
  • Crunchbase: 2,000-3,000 companies with contact info
  • Total: ~20,000-25,000 verified prospects

Organize into Three Vertical CSVs:

  1. Agency/White-Label (Targeting boutique agencies seeking cold email skills)
  2. SaaS/Tech (Targeting 10-500 employee SaaS companies)
  3. Other (Staffing, Logistics, Fintech, Enterprise)

Each vertical gets 4-email sequences pre-written.

Month 2: Website + Marketing Materials

Build Simple Website:

  • Home page: "Cold Email Agency for [Your Country]"
  • Case studies: 2-3 example results (real or anonymized previous work)
  • Services page: Micro/Standard/Premium pricing clearly stated
  • Blog: Republish/adapt from imisofts guides
  • Contact: Simple form or Calendly link

Create Marketing Assets:

  • Cold email templates (3-5 variations by vertical)
  • Case study template (results, timeline, metrics)
  • Pricing one-pager
  • FAQ document

Messaging:

  • "We've helped [X] European B2B companies generate [Y] qualified leads through cold email"
  • Focus on compliance, deliverability, conversion
  • Emphasize regulatory knowledge (UK PECR, German UWG, Dutch single opt-in)

Month 3: Pilot Campaigns + Social Proof

Find 3-5 Pilot Clients:

  • Target: Early-stage SaaS founders or small agencies
  • Offer: Discounted rate (50% off standard price)
  • Deliverable: 30-day managed campaign, daily reporting
  • Goal: Generate 3-5 case studies with real results

Expected Pilot Results (Conservative):

  • 5,000-email campaign
  • 4-8% response rate = 200-400 responses
  • 1-3% meeting booking = 50-120 booked calls
  • 5-10% closing rate = 2-12 new customers

Pricing for Pilots:

  • Standard: €600/month
  • Pilot discount: €300/month
  • Break-even at 2-3 successful pilots with real results

Month 4: Sales + Productization

Sales Plan:

  • Outreach to 500-1,000 European B2B companies
  • Use 26,500-contact master list as foundation
  • Cold email YOUR OWN SERVICES (dogfooding)
  • Target: 10-20 customers by end of month

Productize Your Service:

  • Develop standard workflows (list building, template creation, warmup setup)
  • Create checklist for onboarding (removes custom work)
  • Build email template library (10-20 by vertical)
  • Document campaign process (makes scaling possible)

Month 5: Scaling + Team Building

Hire First Support Person:

  • Handle client onboarding, reporting, basic optimization
  • Part-time (20 hours/week) initially
  • Cost: €500-800/month

Expand Customer Base:

  • Referrals from initial customers (best source)
  • Content marketing (publish 1-2 blog posts weekly)
  • LinkedIn outreach (direct cold email to your ICP)
  • Target: 25-40 total customers

Month 6: White-Label Expansion

Develop White-Label Version:

  • Rebrand materials for partner agencies
  • Offer: €200-400/customer referral margin (agencies resell at €600+)
  • Partner with 10-20 agencies (not direct competitors)
  • Target: Add 20-30 white-label customers

Expected MRR at Month 6:

  • Direct customers: 30-40 × €600 = €18K-24K
  • White-label customers: 20-30 × €300 = €6K-9K
  • Total MRR: €24K-33K (€288K-396K ARR)

Building the Flywheel: From €288K to €2M ARR

Stage 1 (Months 1-6): Solo Founder

  • 30-40 direct customers
  • €24K-33K MRR
  • Role: Service delivery, sales, marketing
  • Hiring: None yet

Stage 2 (Months 7-18): Add Operator

  • Hire operations/delivery person (€1,500-2,000/month)
  • Scale to 100+ direct customers
  • 50+ white-label customers
  • €60K-80K MRR (€720K-960K ARR)

Stage 3 (Months 19-36): Add SDR/Sales

  • Hire junior sales/SDR (€1,500-1,800/month)
  • Scale to 150-200 direct customers
  • 100-150 white-label customers
  • €90K-130K MRR (€1.08M-1.56M ARR)

Stage 4 (Months 37+): Stable €2M+ ARR

  • 200+ direct customers at €600/month
  • 200+ white-label customers at €300/month
  • 3-4 team members
  • €120K+ MRR (€1.44M+ ARR)
  • Potential acquisition target for larger agencies

Your Competitive Advantages

1. Regulatory Expertise

  • UK PECR (opt-out)
  • German UWG Section 7 (double opt-in with exceptions)
  • Netherlands single opt-in
  • Ireland (opt-out)
  • Competitors don't know this stuff

2. Pre-Built Infrastructure

  • 26,500+ qualified European leads already scraped
  • 3 vertical CSVs ready to deploy
  • 4-email sequences pre-written per vertical
  • Cut client onboarding from 4 weeks to 1 week

3. White-Label Ready

  • Imisofts can provide white-label infrastructure
  • You handle sales and customer success
  • They handle campaigns and deliverability
  • 40-50% margin on white-label volume

4. Underserved Markets

  • UK: 23-30 agencies (vs. 500+ in US)
  • Germany: <10 (strict compliance = moat)
  • Netherlands: <5
  • Ireland: 15-20 (easy to displace with specialization)
  • Sweden/Finland: <3

Tools You'll Need

  • Email Platform: Instantly (white-label capability)
  • List Building: Apollo (European filters)
  • CRM: Close or HubSpot (client dashboard)
  • Reporting: Google Data Studio (automated reports)
  • Website: WordPress or Webflow
  • Domain: Namecheap or GoDaddy

Total Monthly Cost: €300-600 (software licenses)

Realistic Timeline to Profitability

  • Month 1-2: Break-even ($0 revenue, €300 costs)
  • Month 3: €1-2K revenue (pilots)
  • Month 4: €6-10K revenue (first paying customers)
  • Month 5: €12-18K revenue (customer growth)
  • Month 6: €24-33K revenue (operational leverage kicking in)
  • Month 12: €60-90K MRR (€720K-1.08M ARR)

Total investment to reach €100K MRR: €5-10K (software + domain + initial contractor help).

Common Mistakes

  1. Targeting US market (saturated)
  • Europe has 10x less competition. Focus there.
  1. Not specializing by country
  • "European agency" is too broad. Pick UK or Germany. Own it.
  1. Ignoring regulatory differences
  • Compliance becomes your selling point. Learn it deeply.
  1. Trying to deliver everything manually
  • Productize early. Create workflows, templates, checklists. Scale without hiring.
  1. Pricing too low
  • €300-600/month is standard, not cheap. Charge it. Focus on results.

Your Action Plan

  1. This week: Choose primary market (UK or Germany)
  2. Next 2 weeks: Build master list of 25,000 prospects (Apollo + LinkedIn)
  3. Week 3-4: Create website and cold email templates
  4. Month 2: Outreach to 50 pilot customers with heavy discount
  5. Month 3: Deliver on pilots, generate case studies
  6. Month 4: Full-price sales to 10-20 standard customers
  7. Month 5: Scale to 40+ customers, add white-label
  8. Month 6: Hire operator, expand to €30K+ MRR

Europe is underserved. The opportunity is massive. The question is: will you move first?

Market cap potential: €500K-€2M ARR within 36 months. That's a real business.
