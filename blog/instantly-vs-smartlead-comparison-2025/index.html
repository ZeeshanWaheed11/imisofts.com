Deliverability Features: The Technical Edge

Deliverability determines whether your emails reach the inbox or disappear into spam folders. Here's where the platforms truly differentiate themselves.

Instantly.ai's Deliverability Arsenal

  • Smart IP Rotation: Automatically distributes sending across multiple IPs to maintain sender reputation
  • Inbox Rotation: Cycles through email accounts intelligently to prevent burnout
  • Real-time Blacklist Monitoring: Alerts you instantly if any sending domain gets flagged
  • Advanced Warmup Pool: Over 200,000 real inboxes in the warmup network
  • ESP Matching: Sends from Gmail to Gmail, Outlook to Outlook for better deliverability

Smartlead.ai's Approach

  • Standard Warmup: Basic email warmup with smaller pool size
  • Spintax Support: Good for creating email variations
  • Basic Rotation: Account rotation without intelligent load balancing
  • Manual IP Management: Requires more hands-on configuration

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use both platforms simultaneously?

Yes, but it's not recommended. Managing campaigns across two platforms increases complexity and costs. Pick one based on your needs: Instantly for general cold email, Smartlead only if you need complex branching sequences.

Q: How many email accounts do I need?

For optimal deliverability, use 1 email account per 30-50 emails/day. For 1,000 emails/day, you need 20-30 accounts. Instantly's unlimited accounts make this affordable at scale.

Q: What about email warmup?

Both platforms include warmup, but Instantly's network is larger (200,000+ inboxes vs 50,000+). Warm up accounts for at least 14-21 days before sending campaigns.

Q: Which platform is better for agencies?

Instantly is better for agencies due to unlimited accounts, white-label options (enterprise plan), and easier client onboarding. We manage 50+ client campaigns on Instantly.

Q: Can I migrate from one platform to another?

Yes, but you'll need to re-warm your email accounts. Export your leads from the old platform and import to the new one. The migration typically takes 2-3 weeks including warmup.
