Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our web development services.

How long does it take to build a website?

Timeline depends on project scope. Landing pages take 1-2 weeks, multi-page business websites 3-4 weeks, and complex web applications 6-12 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the discovery call based on your specific requirements.

How much does web development cost?

Our projects range from $3,000 for landing pages to $50,000+ for enterprise applications. Most business websites fall in the $5,000-$15,000 range. We provide fixed-price quotes after understanding your goals, so there are no surprise costs.

What technologies do you use for web development?

We use modern, production-ready technologies: React, Next.js, Vue.js for frontends; Node.js, Python, PHP for backends; PostgreSQL, MongoDB for databases; and AWS, Vercel, or DigitalOcean for hosting. Technology choices are made based on your project requirements, not trends.

Do you provide hosting and ongoing maintenance?

Yes. We offer managed hosting packages starting at $99/month that include security updates, performance monitoring, backups, and minor content updates. You can also self-hostâ€”we provide full documentation and handover support.

Will I own my website and code after the project?

Yes. Upon final payment, you receive complete ownership of all code, design files, and assets. We provide full source code access and documentation. There's no vendor lock-inâ€”you can host anywhere and hire anyone to maintain it.

Do you build mobile-responsive websites?

Every website we build is fully responsive across all devicesâ€”desktop, tablet, and mobile. Mobile-first design is standard, not an add-on. We test on real devices and optimize for touch interactions and mobile performance.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Yes. We specialize in both new builds and redesigns. For redesigns, we audit your current site, preserve SEO value and content, then rebuild with modern design and improved performance. We can also migrate from platforms like WordPress, Wix, or Squarespace.

Do you optimize websites for search engines (SEO)?

Yes. Technical SEO is built into every project: fast load times, proper meta tags, structured data markup, semantic HTML, mobile optimization, and Core Web Vitals compliance. For ongoing SEO campaigns and content strategy, we partner with specialists or provide referrals.

What is your development process?

Our process: 1) Discovery call to understand goals and requirements, 2) Proposal with scope, timeline, and fixed price, 3) Design mockups for approval, 4) Development with weekly progress updates, 5) Testing and revisions, 6) Launch and handover. You're involved at every stage.

Can you integrate with third-party tools and APIs?

Yes. We integrate with payment processors (Stripe, PayPal), CRMs (HubSpot, Salesforce), email platforms (Mailchimp, SendGrid), analytics (GA4, Mixpanel), and custom APIs. If there's an API, we can connect it to your website.
