## How to Use the CSV Files

### Step 1: Import to Instantly

1. Create account at Instantly.ai
2. Go to Campaigns → Create Campaign

### Step 2: Configure Email Sequences

1. Select one of the 4 pre-written emails per vertical
2. Customize subject line (add company name, metric)
3. Customize body (add company-specific reference)
4. Set sending schedule: 50-100 emails/day
5. Enable warmup: 2-3 week ramp-up before aggressive sending

### Step 3: Set Sending Schedule

**Conservative Approach (Recommended):**
- Week 1: 50 emails/day (from CSV)
- Week 2: 75 emails/day
- Week 3: 100 emails/day
- Week 4+: 150-200 emails/day (if deliverability stays strong)

**Aggressive Approach (Only if domain warm):**
- Week 1: 100 emails/day
- Week 2: 150 emails/day
- Week 3+: 200-300 emails/day

**Monitors:** Keep complaint rate <0.1% and bounce rate <5%

### Step 4: Campaign Reporting

**Track Per Vertical:**
- Open rate target: 15-20%
- Click rate target: 8-14%
- Response rate target: 3-8%
- Meeting booking rate: 1-3%

**Optimize Based on Response:**
- Top-performing email? Scale it.
- Low response? Switch to next email in sequence.
- Country variation? Create country-specific sequences.

## Tips for Success

### Tip 1: Localize by Country

- UK/Ireland: English fine
- Netherlands: Use Dutch for Subject + Opening (5.8% response vs 2.4%)
- Germany: Use German for Subject + Opening
- France/Spain/Italy: Consider local language for 15-20% uplift

### Tip 2: Segment by Industry Within Vertical

- Vertical 2 (SaaS) breaks into: Fintech, HR Tech, Logistics, MarTech
- Each has unique pain points
- Customize email 1 per segment

### Tip 3: A/B Test Subject Lines

- Test 5 subject variations per vertical
- Send 100 emails per variation
- Identify winner
- Scale winner to full list

### Tip 4: Monitor Deliverability

- Complaint rate <0.1% (ISP tolerance threshold)
- Bounce rate <5%
- If exceeded, slow domain warmup

### Tip 5: Book Calls, Not Just Leads

- Response rate matters less than meeting booking rate
- Optimize for: "Are you open to a 15-min call?"
- Not: "Want to learn more?"
