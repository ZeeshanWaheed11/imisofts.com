FAQ_SCHEMA:
Q: Can I use these templates exactly as-is?
A: Not recommended. Replace [placeholders] with your data and add spintax variations (2-3 options per section). Identical emails trigger spam filters.

Q: Should I adjust templates for my specific company?
A: Yes. Change the opening line to reference their specific achievement or challenge. Generic templates don't work. Specific personalization is key.

Q: How many times should I test a template before scaling?
A: Test on 50-100 prospects per industry first. Measure reply rate. If you're above 1.5%, you can scale. If below 1%, optimize the subject line or opening line first.

Q: Can I combine templates from different industries?
A: Not recommended. Industries have different pain points, CTAs, and messaging frameworks. Use the template for your industry as-is.

Q: Where do I find the data to fill in [placeholders]?
A: LinkedIn (job changes, company news), Crunchbase (funding), company websites (product launches), Google News (press releases). Use Clay or Apollo for automation.
