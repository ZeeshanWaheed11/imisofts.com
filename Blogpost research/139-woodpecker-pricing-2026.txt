# Woodpecker Pricing (2026): Plans, Limits & Cost Breakdown

Woodpecker has always positioned itself as "the simple alternative to complex cold email tools."

Their 2026 pricing reflects that philosophy: dead simple, but with real constraints built in. It's not a per-contact model like Apollo, not a per-user model like Lemlist. It's per-prospect. And that changes everything about the unit economics.

## Woodpecker Pricing Plans 2026

Woodpecker charges based on the number of prospects (contacts) in your campaigns, not the number of emails you send.

**Starter Plan**: $49/month
- 500 prospects maximum
- Unlimited sequences
- Unlimited emails per prospect
- Basic integrations
- Email warm-up included
- Deliverability stats
- No contact limits per sequence
- 1 user

**Professional Plan**: $149/month
- 2,500 prospects maximum
- Everything in Starter
- Advanced integrations (Zapier, webhooks)
- Priority support
- CRM integrations
- Multi-user (up to 5)

**Business Plan**: $249/month
- 5,000 prospects maximum
- Everything in Professional
- API access
- White-label options (on request)
- Dedicated support

**Custom Plan**: 10,000+ prospects
- Custom pricing (usually $500-1,000/month)
- Everything in Business
- SLA guarantees
- Technical onboarding

There's also a **Free Plan** with 50 prospects, perfect for testing.

## Understanding Woodpecker's Pricing Model: Per-Prospect, Not Per-Email

This is the critical difference between Woodpecker and every competitor.

You buy a "seat" of X prospects. Once you reach that limit, you either delete old prospects or upgrade.

**Example**:
- Starter plan: 500 prospects
- You add 500 contacts to a campaign
- Plan is "full"
- To add 50 more, you must delete 50 old ones or upgrade to Professional

This is different from:
- **Instantly**: Unlimited contacts, pay for tool
- **SmartLead**: Unlimited contacts, pay per user
- **Lemlist**: Unlimited contacts, pay per user

Woodpecker's model incentivizes smaller campaigns and active management.

## Cost Per Email & Cost Per Prospect Analysis

Here's where things get interesting.

**Starter ($49/month with 500 prospects)**:
- If you send 100 emails per prospect (5 follow-ups × 20 attempts) = 50,000 emails
- Cost per email: $0.00098
- Cost per prospect: $0.098

**Professional ($149/month with 2,500 prospects)**:
- If you send 50,000 emails = 50,000 emails
- Cost per email: $0.00298
- Cost per prospect: $0.0596

**Business ($249/month with 5,000 prospects)**:
- If you send 100,000 emails = 100,000 emails
- Cost per email: $0.00249
- Cost per prospect: $0.0498

Cost-per-email looks cheap. But that's misleading because you're limited by number of prospects, not sends.

The real comparison is **cost-per-prospect**:

- **Woodpecker Business**: $249 ÷ 5,000 = $0.0498 per prospect
- **Instantly**: $359 ÷ 50,000 (realistic monthly volume) = $0.00718 per prospect
- **SmartLead**: $99 ÷ 10,000 (realistic volume) = $0.0099 per prospect

On a per-prospect basis, Woodpecker is 5-7x more expensive than alternatives.

## The Sequence Advantage: Why Woodpecker Wins on Follow-ups

Here's Woodpecker's real selling point: they don't limit sequences.

**Woodpecker sequences**: Unlimited follow-ups within the 5,000 prospect limit

**Instantly sequences**: Limited to 3-5 follow-ups total (sending becomes restricted beyond that)

**SmartLead sequences**: Effectively limited by send volume/day caps

If you're running 5 email sequences to the same 5,000 prospects:
- Woodpecker: 5,000 prospects × 5 sequences = 25,000 emails total (included)
- Instantly: Would cost more (higher volume, hits send limits)
- SmartLead: Would cost more (requires higher tier or multiple users)

Woodpecker's model makes sense for **high follow-up intensity**.

## Real-World Comparison: Same Campaign, Three Tools

Let's say you're running a campaign:
- 2,000 prospects
- 5-email sequence
- 10,000 total emails
- 30-day duration

**Woodpecker Professional**:
- Cost: $149/month
- Prospect slots used: 2,000/2,500 (80% capacity)
- Follow-ups: Unlimited
- Can run 5+ sequences simultaneously
- Annual cost: $1,788

**Instantly (1 seat)**:
- Cost: $359/month
- Prospect limits: None
- Follow-ups: Limited (3-5 max practically)
- Can run multiple sequences
- Annual cost: $4,308

**SmartLead (1 user)**:
- Cost: $99/month
- Prospect limits: None
- Follow-ups: 5+ possible
- Can run multiple sequences
- Send limit: 500-1,000/day depending on warmup
- Annual cost: $1,188

**Winner**: SmartLead is cheapest. But Woodpecker is competitive if you're doing heavy sequences.

## Where Woodpecker Gets Expensive: Campaign Scaling

The problem emerges when you want to scale.

Say you want to run **4 separate 2,000 prospect campaigns simultaneously**:

**Woodpecker**:
- Business plan: 5,000 prospects (can fit 2-3 campaigns)
- Need custom plan for 8,000 total: ~$500-800/month
- Annual: $6,000-9,600

**Instantly**:
- 1 user: $359/month (can run 4 campaigns easily)
- Annual: $4,308

**SmartLead**:
- 2 users: $99 + $79 (team seat) = $178/month
- Annual: $2,136

On scaling, Woodpecker becomes expensive fast.

## Woodpecker's Sweet Spot: Small Teams, High Follow-up Intensity

Woodpecker makes the most sense for:

**Single operator doing deep sequences**
- 1 campaign of 1,000 prospects
- 5+ email sequences per prospect
- 5,000+ total emails/month
- Heavy follow-up work
- Cost: $49/month (Starter)

Cost-per-email: $0.01, which is competitive. And you're fully in control of the sequences without worrying about hitting API limits or send-rate caps.

**Agencies managing multiple small campaigns**
- 5 campaigns × 500 prospects each = 2,500 total prospects
- Professional plan: $149/month
- Total team cost: $149/month for up to 5 users

Cost per team member: $30/month. Incredibly cheap for small teams running conservative campaigns.

**Mid-market companies with short sales cycles**
- 2,000 prospects
- 3-email sequence
- Quick follow-up needed
- Professional plan: $149/month

The speed of execution (no API throttling, no send-rate limits) makes Woodpecker appealing despite higher per-prospect cost.

## Where Woodpecker Loses to Competitors

**High-volume outreach**:
- If you want to send 20,000+ emails/month, Woodpecker's prospect limit makes it inefficient
- Instantly ($359/month) unlimited is better value

**Complex automation**:
- If you need conditional logic, dynamic content, webhook triggers
- Instantly's n8n integration or SmartLead's advanced features are superior
- Woodpecker's automation is basic

**Enterprise features**:
- If you need white-label, advanced analytics, SLAs
- Outreach or SalesLoft ($1,000+/month) are designed for this
- Woodpecker's custom plan is not competitive

**Multi-channel campaigns**:
- If you're doing email + SMS + LinkedIn
- Woodpecker is email-only
- You need best-of-breed per channel

## Woodpecker vs Instantly: The Real Comparison

These two tools compete most directly.

| Feature | Woodpecker | Instantly |
|---------|-----------|-----------|
| **Pricing** | $49-249/month | $359/month |
| **Contact limit** | 500-5,000 | Unlimited |
| **Email sequences** | Unlimited | 3-5 practical limit |
| **Cost-per-prospect** | $0.05-0.10 | $0.007-0.01 |
| **Send-rate limits** | None (within prospect limit) | Rate-limited per warmup |
| **Setup difficulty** | Low | Medium |
| **Automation** | Basic | Advanced (n8n) |
| **Multi-user** | Included (up to 5) | Per seat |
| **Best for** | Deep sequences, small teams | Volume, complex workflows |

**Choose Woodpecker if**: You're doing 5+ follow-ups per prospect and want simple setup.

**Choose Instantly if**: You're sending high volume and need workflow automation.

## Woodpecker's Real Problem: Growth Ceiling

Woodpecker works great until you need to scale.

Once you hit 5,000 prospects and want to add more, you're forced into custom pricing. At that point, you should reconsider:

- **Instantly**: $359/month unlimited (cheaper than custom Woodpecker)
- **SmartLead**: $99/month per user unlimited (add users as needed)
- **Private server**: $489/year (full control, zero limits)

## The Honest Take on Woodpecker Pricing

Woodpecker is a **nice tool for small operations**, not a growth tool.

**Best for**:
- Solopreneurs testing cold email ($49/month)
- Small agencies with <5 campaigns ($149/month)
- Teams focused on follow-up intensity, not volume

**Not for**:
- Scaling to 10,000+ prospects
- High-volume enterprise campaigns
- Teams that need flexible capacity

**Price positioning**: Mid-market between free tools and enterprise platforms. But not the cheapest at any scale.

## FAQ Schema

**Q: Is Woodpecker cheaper than Instantly?**
A: For small campaigns (500 prospects), yes—Woodpecker is $49/month vs Instantly at $359/month. But at scale (5,000+ prospects), Instantly is cheaper because Woodpecker's custom pricing becomes expensive.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my prospect limit in Woodpecker?**
A: You must delete old prospects to make room for new ones, or upgrade your plan. This makes it impractical for large-scale operations that want to maintain historical data.

**Q: Does Woodpecker charge per email or per prospect?**
A: Per prospect. You buy a plan with 500-5,000 prospect slots. Once full, you can't add more until you delete old prospects or upgrade.

**Q: Can I use one Woodpecker account for multiple campaigns?**
A: Yes, but each campaign counts toward your prospect limit. A Business plan with 5,000 prospects could be 5 × 1,000 prospect campaigns or 1 × 5,000 prospect campaign.

**Q: Is email warm-up included in Woodpecker?**
A: Yes, all paid plans include warm-up functionality. You don't need to buy it separately like on some competitor platforms.
