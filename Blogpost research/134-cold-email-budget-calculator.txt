## The Calculator Framework

**Step 1: What's Your Monthly Email Volume?**

- Startup: 10K-25K emails/month

**Step 2: What's Your Team Size?**

- Solo founder: 1 person

- Growing team: 5-10 people

**Step 3: What's Your Deal Size?**

- Small ($1K-5K): Tight margin business
- Medium ($5K-20K): Healthy margin SaaS
- Large ($20K-100K): Enterprise products
- Mega ($100K+): High-ticket B2B

## The Cost-Per-Customer Reality Check

Your budget only makes sense if cost per customer < deal size / 3.

If deal is $30K, your max spend should be $10K per customer (1/3 of deal value).

| Budget Tier | Cost Per Customer | Works For Deal Size |
|------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Startup ($250) | $250 | $10K+ |
| Growth ($1,550) | $124 | $5K+ |
| Scale ($3,500) | $97 | $3K+ |
| Enterprise ($17,800) | $59 | $1K+ |

If your deal is $2K:
- Startup tier: Works (250/2K = 12.5% customer acquisition cost)
- Growth tier: Doesn't work (124/2K = 6% CAC, but high overhead)
- Scale: Doesn't work (97/2K = 5% CAC + fixed costs)
- Enterprise: Definitely doesn't work

Choose Startup tier only for $2K products.

**Validate with ROI:**
Cost per customer = (Monthly budget × 12) / (Monthly emails × reply% × conversion%)

If CPL > deal size/3, downgrade tier.
If CPL < deal size/10, upgrade tier (you're underinvesting).

## FAQ Schema

**Q: How do I know what budget tier is right for me?**

A: Calculate cost per customer (annual budget / expected monthly customers). If it's less than 10% of deal value, you're likely underfunding. If it's more than 30%, you're overfunding. Target 15-20% CAC.

**Q: Can I start with Startup tier and upgrade?**

A: Yes. Most companies start at Startup ($300/mo), validate ROI, then move to Growth ($1,500/mo). Upgrade path is normal.

**Q: What if I can't afford my tier?**

A: Your deal size is too small for cold email ROI. Or your margins are too tight. Either increase deal size, improve your product's margin, or use cheaper marketing (organic, referral).

**Q: How much should I budget for hidden costs?**

A: Add 20% contingency to your calculated budget for unexpected tools, scaling, team expansion. $1,500 budget should be $1,800 in practice.

**Q: When should I move from Growth to Scale tier?**

A: When Growth tier volume (50K emails) reaches 70-80% of monthly capacity and cost per customer improves <15%. That's your signal to scale infrastructure and team.
