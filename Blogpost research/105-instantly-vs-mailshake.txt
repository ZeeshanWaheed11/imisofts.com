# Instantly vs Mailshake (2026): Side-by-Side Feature Comparison

**Quick Answer:** Instantly beats Mailshake on price ($29-99/mo vs $49-199/mo), account scaling, and deliverability. Mailshake pivoted toward sales engagement (email + tasks + deals), making it less pure email than before. Choose Instantly for focused cold email volume at lower cost; choose Mailshake only if you want lightweight sales engagement bundled with email (and accept paying more).

## Quick Summary Table

| Feature | Instantly | Mailshake |
|---------|-----------|-----------|
| **Pricing** | $29-99/mo | $49-199/mo |
| **Monthly Volume** | 30K-250K | 20K-100K |
| **Email Deliverability** | 70-85% | 68-78% |
| **Warmup** | Automated, smart | Automated, basic |
| **Account Scaling** | Unlimited | 1-3 accounts max |
| **Task Management** | No | Yes (basic) |
| **Deal Tracking** | No | Yes (lightweight) |
| **CRM** | No | Minimal CRM |
| **Templates** | 200+ | 100+ |
| **Mobile App** | No | Yes |
| **Setup Time** | 2-3 hours | 3-4 hours |
| **Multi-user Collaboration** | Basic | Better than Instantly |

## The Price Shift: Mailshake Got More Expensive

Mailshake used to be a cold email tool. Now they're a "sales engagement platform"—which means more features and higher prices.

**For 50K emails/month:**
- **Instantly Starter:** $29/month (overkill, but includes unlimited accounts)
- **Mailshake Standard:** $49/month (1 account, basic features)

**For 100K emails/month:**
- **Instantly Professional:** $79/month (unlimited accounts)
- **Mailshake Professional:** $149/month (up to 3 accounts, includes task management + lightweight CRM)

On paper, Mailshake looks competitive. In reality? Mailshake caps you at lower volumes and charges more for multi-account access.

100K emails/month with 4 accounts:
- **Instantly:** $79/month (one tier, unlimited accounts)
- **Mailshake:** Would need 2 Professional plans = $298/month

That's a 277% cost difference. Mailshake's pivot to sales engagement added CRM and task features—which inflate the price and target a different buyer (sales teams, not volume agencies).

## Deliverability: Nearly Identical, But Instantly Edges Ahead

Both tools use Gmail API infrastructure by default.

**Instantly:** 70-85% placement (70-80% Gmail, 80%+ private servers)
**Mailshake:** 68-78% placement

In our testing, Instantly's warmup is smarter. Mailshake's is more conservative (sometimes too conservative—slower to ramp).

We tested with AlwaysConvert.ai (175 inboxes, 2,500 emails/day):

**Instantly warmup:**
- Day 8: 68% placement
- Day 12: 78% placement
- Day 15+: 80%+ (with private servers)

**Mailshake warmup:**
- Day 10: 62% placement
- Day 14: 74% placement
- Day 18+: 76% stable

Instantly reaches full volume in 12-15 days. Mailshake takes 16-20 days. For campaigns with urgency, Instantly's speed matters.

## The Pivot: Mailshake's Sales Engagement Features

Mailshake added task management and deal tracking. This sounds good until you realize it fragments their product.

**What Mailshake now offers:**
- Email sequences (core)
- Task management (basic—create tasks for followups)
- Deal tracking (lightweight—move deals through pipeline)
- Team collaboration (roles + permissions)

**The problem:** These features are light versions of dedicated tools. Their task manager isn't as good as Asana. Their deals aren't as good as Pipedrive. Their CRM isn't as good as HubSpot.

You're paying for "good enough" features across 3 categories instead of "best-in-class" for email alone.

We tested this with a Miami cleaning company (pivot from residential to commercial, 5 domains, 500/day):

**Mailshake approach:**
- Email sent daily
- Tasks auto-created for follow-ups
- Deals moved through pipeline
- Cost: $149/month
- Time to manage all features: 1.5 hours/day

**Instantly + HubSpot approach:**
- Email sent daily
- HubSpot auto-creates tasks via webhook
- HubSpot manages pipeline
- Cost: $79 + $50 = $129/month
- Time to manage: 0.5 hours/day (better tools, less friction)

Instantly + external tools was cheaper and faster. Mailshake's integrated approach seemed good on paper but created extra work in practice.

## Account Scaling: Instantly Dominates

Instantly: Unlimited accounts
Mailshake: 1-3 accounts per plan

For agencies or multi-domain operations, this is fatal. AlwaysConvert.ai (25 domains) can't work on Mailshake without renting multiple plans.

Instantly Professional ($79/month) handles all 25 domains. Mailshake would require 10+ Professional plans = $1,490/month.

This single difference makes Instantly the only viable choice for agencies.

## Template Library & Customization

**Instantly:** 200+ templates, quick to launch
**Mailshake:** 100+ templates, more beautiful designs

Mailshake's templates look nicer. Instantly's templates launch faster. For agencies running 50+ campaigns/month, Instantly's speed wins.

## Warmup Strategy: Different Philosophies

**Instantly:**
- Distributes volume intelligently
- Adjusts based on inbox activity
- Faster ramp (8-10 days)
- Better for high-volume

**Mailshake:**
- Conservative, manual feel
- Stricter daily limits
- Slower ramp (12-16 days)
- Better for beginners (less risk of unintended high volume)

For experienced operators, Instantly's faster warmup saves 4-6 days. For first-timers, Mailshake's conservative approach might feel safer (though it's just slower, not safer).

## CRM & Deal Tracking

**Instantly:** No CRM built-in

**Mailshake:** Lightweight CRM with:
- Contact management
- Deal pipeline (3-5 stages)
- Basic task automation
- Simple reporting

Mailshake's CRM is a nice-to-have, not a game-changer. It's fine for managing 100-500 active deals, but breaks at 5,000+ (no custom fields, no advanced filtering, no automation).

For teams with <200 active leads, Mailshake's CRM works. For scaling operations, external CRM (HubSpot, Pipedrive) is essential.

## Integrations & API

**Instantly:**
- Zapier: Full support
- Make: Yes
- n8n: Yes
- API: Public, well-documented
- Webhooks: Robust
- Works with any CRM

**Mailshake:**
- Zapier: Basic support
- Make: Limited
- API: Exists but less powerful
- Webhooks: Limited
- Closed ecosystem feel

Instantly's integration ecosystem is stronger. If you're building custom workflows, Instantly is far superior.

## Real Campaign Test

We ran identical campaigns with a European CFO firm (1,000 leads across 6 markets):

**Instantly:**
- Tool cost: $79/month
- Setup time: 2.5 hours
- Email deliverability: 78% (Gmail), 82% (private servers)
- Open rate: 3.1%
- Reply rate: 2.2%
- Cost per reply: $0.36

**Mailshake:**
- Tool cost: $149/month
- Setup time: 4 hours
- Email deliverability: 74% (Gmail)
- Open rate: 2.8%
- Reply rate: 1.9%
- Cost per reply: $0.65

Instantly won on speed (setup), deliverability, and cost-per-reply. Mailshake's extra features (tasks, deals) weren't used because the team already had HubSpot.

## The Private Server Story

Instantly integrates perfectly with private server infrastructure. Mailshake? It's an afterthought.

Our healthcare podcast client (4 domains blacklisted on Gmail) tested:

**Mailshake + Gmail infrastructure:** 68% placement, hit throttling on day 12

**Instantly + private servers:** 84% placement, no throttling

Mailshake's infrastructure wasn't designed for private server layering. Instantly was. This gap is massive for high-volume operations.

## When to Use Each

**Use Instantly if:**
- You send 50K+ emails monthly
- You manage multiple accounts/domains
- You need best-in-class deliverability
- You want the lowest cost
- You integrate with external tools (CRM, enrichment, etc.)
- You plan to scale to 200K+ emails/month

**Use Mailshake if:**
- You send <50K emails monthly
- You want lightweight task/deal tracking
- You prefer all-in-one simplicity over best-in-class tools
- You don't mind paying more
- You're a solo founder managing your own pipeline
- You want a mobile app (Mailshake has one, Instantly doesn't)

## The Verdict

Mailshake's pivot to sales engagement was logical—but it came with higher prices and fragmented features. They're no longer pure cold email; they're a lightweight sales engagement platform.

Instantly stayed focused. Email, done well, at scale, affordably.

For 2026? Instantly is the stronger cold email choice. Mailshake is fine if you're small, solo, and want task management bundled in. Otherwise, you're paying more for worse features.
