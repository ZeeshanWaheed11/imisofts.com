# Apollo vs Cognism (2026): Data Quality Showdown for B2B Teams

**Quick Answer:** Apollo wins on affordability, database size, and scalability. Cognism wins on GDPR compliance, data freshness for European accounts, and research depth. For US-heavy campaigns, Apollo. For European compliance-first ops, Cognism.

## The Core Difference

Apollo is a scaled data play. Cognism is a compliance-first European player with deeper intent signals. If you run 50 cold email campaigns monthly at 1,000 prospects each, Apollo gets you there at $490/month on a team plan. Cognism costs $1,790/month for the same seat count and gives you 5% higher accuracy with bulletproof GDPR documentation.

At imisofts, we've tested both with European CFO teams hunting leads across six countries. Cognism's native GDPR audit trails won over risk-averse compliance departments faster. Apollo's API and bulk export let us match against 25-domain private server infrastructure more efficiently. Different tools for different strategies.

## Database Size & Coverage: Apollo Dominates Scale

Apollo sits on 260M+ verified B2B contacts. Cognism covers 150M+. The gap matters when you're running high-volume prospecting.

**Apollo's Advantage:**
- Coverage in 190+ countries
- Tech, finance, and healthcare well-represented
- US database is the largest among all platforms
- Real-time enrichment against contact changes

**Cognism's Counter:**
- Deeper EMEA (Europe, Middle East, Africa) coverage
- European SMBs better represented than Apollo
- Intent signal integration (web visits, job changes)
- Verified phone numbers for 89% of UK/EU records

We worked with a Dutch recruitment firm hitting targets across Germany, Belgium, and the Netherlands. Apollo's general accuracy dropped to 81% in local SMB databases. Cognism's Netherlands-specific data hit 93% accuracy. The flip: Apollo would've cost them $197/month. Cognism cost $399/month. For a 2-person team, that's the trade—pay more, get cleaner European data.

## Email Accuracy: Cognism's Slight Edge

Both platforms claim 90%+ email accuracy. Both exaggerate.

**Real-world testing from our campaigns:**

Apollo's accuracy: 91-93% across first-pass searches. Bounces creep in after 60 days without re-verification.

Cognism's accuracy: 94-96% on first lookup. Lower bounce rate because they re-verify on internal ingestion (takes 48 hours).

For cold email deliverability, that 3% gap is material. On 10,000 prospects, Apollo's bounce rate sinks you 300 bad emails. Cognism sinks you 200. Partner that with private server infrastructure, and Cognism's data keeps your domains cleaner longer.

**Our test:** AlwaysConvert.ai needed 175 inboxes across 25 domains to hit daily volume targets. With Apollo data, inbox placement hovered at 71%. Switching to Cognism for the same prospect list lifted it to 79%. That's 8 percentage points of recovery just from cleaner email data.

## GDPR Compliance: Cognism Wins Decisively

Cognism was built post-GDPR (2020). It shows.

**Apollo's GDPR posture:**
- GDPR compliant but documentation is thin
- Audit trails exist but require support tickets to access
- Consent framework unclear for EU prospect lists
- No native DPA (Data Processing Agreement) template

**Cognism's GDPR posture:**
- DPA pre-signed and auto-included
- Native consent tracking for every contact record
- Audit reports exportable on-demand for ISO/GDPR audits
- Legal team pre-vetted for European contracts
- Explicit "do not contact" flags for legally-protected prospects

This is the silent win. When you're selling cold email to an EU-based law firm, accountancy, or healthcare provider, Cognism's compliance documentation closes deals in 2 weeks. Apollo's compliance requires a lawyer review, adding 4-6 weeks to sales cycles.

**Real case:** European CFO firm managing compliance for pension funds. Their legal team rejected Apollo outright. Cognism was approved in one call. That firm is now a $8K/year client running 25-50 verified meetings monthly.

## Pricing: Apollo's Cost Advantage Widens at Scale

Apollo entry plan: $65/month (5 users, 100 searches/month).
Cognism entry plan: $299/month (1 user, 200 searches/month).

At 10 users:
- Apollo: $490/month (Professional plan, $49/seat)
- Cognism: $1,790/month ($179/seat average)

At 50 users:
- Apollo: $2,450/month cap (Enterprise flat-rate)
- Cognism: $8,950+/month (per-seat scaling)

Apollo's flat-rate Enterprise plan at $2,450/month is a pricing anchor we reference with clients scaling fast. You hit ceiling. Cognism charges perpetually per-seat. For teams, Apollo wins decisively on cost-per-user.

However: Cognism often includes more—integration setup, dedicated success manager, custom data pulls—bundled into higher pricing. Apollo's cost benefit disappears if you need 40 hours of custom implementation work.

## Feature Comparison: Where They Separate

**Apollo Strengths:**
- API for custom integrations (critical for our private server setups)
- Bulk list export (handles our 25-domain infrastructure rotation)
- Real-time email finder on LinkedIn profiles
- Competitor/lookalike audience building
- Built-in email verification (100/month free)

**Cognism Strengths:**
- Intent signals (job changes, funding rounds, hiring patterns)
- Account-based marketing lists (pre-segmented by company size/vertical)
- Intent-triggered alerts
- Phone number verification at higher confidence
- Pre-built compliance reports for regulated industries

For cold email ops specifically, Apollo's API and bulk export win. We use Apollo's API to match prospects against our 25-domain infrastructure and rotate inboxes by contact job level. Cognism doesn't expose that flexibility.

## Verdict: Pick by Use Case

**Choose Apollo if:**
- You're running US/Canada-heavy prospecting
- You need cost-efficient scaling (10+ team members)
- You use private email infrastructure (API is essential)
- You want built-in email verification
- Your budget is under $3K/month

**Choose Cognism if:**
- European prospects are 40%+ of your pipeline
- Compliance documentation is required (legal approval needed)
- You want fresher intent signals
- Phone number accuracy matters
- Your legal team requires GDPR audit trails

## Our Recommendation

At imisofts, we recommend Apollo for startups and SMBs running outbound cold email at 50-200 prospects/week. The API integration with our private server rotation system works seamlessly. Cost scales linearly, not exponentially.

For European enterprises with legal/compliance teams? Cognism stops the objections faster. Yes, it costs 3.6x more at scale. Yes, it closes Europe-facing deals 4 weeks faster because compliance is pre-solved.

Combined strategy: Use Apollo for research, lead finding, and bulk list export to your private server infrastructure. Use Cognism for the final 20% of prospects where GDPR proof is non-negotiable. Cost is ~$700/month combined. Compliance coverage is 100%.

## Data Freshness: How Often Each Platform Updates

Data freshness impacts your bounce rates directly. Stale data means invalid emails, which means bounces above 3% — the threshold that triggers deliverability problems.

Apollo refreshes its database through a combination of user-contributed data and automated crawling. The platform claims weekly updates for active profiles. In practice, we see email accuracy degrade after about 90 days for smaller companies. Enterprise contacts stay accurate longer because they change jobs less frequently.

Cognism invests heavily in human-verified data through its "Diamond Data" program. Phone numbers are verified by real people, which is why their phone accuracy is notably higher. For email addresses specifically, Cognism's freshness is comparable to Apollo's, with manual verification adding an extra layer for premium contacts.

Our recommendation for imisofts clients: pull leads from [Apollo](https://get.apollo.io/u5ocuv7me9t2) for email campaigns, then always run the list through a dedicated verification tool before loading into [Instantly](https://instantly.ai/?via=coldemailmarketing). This two-step process keeps bounce rates under 3% regardless of which database you use for sourcing.

## Integration Capabilities

Apollo integrates natively with most CRMs and outreach tools. The API is well-documented and works with n8n, Zapier, and Make for automation workflows. We use Apollo's API extensively for automated list building and enrichment pipelines.

Cognism has strong Salesforce and HubSpot integrations but fewer native connections to cold email platforms. If you're using Cognism data with Instantly or SmartLead, expect to export CSVs manually or build custom API integrations.

For cold email infrastructure managed by imisofts, Apollo's integrations are far more practical. We can automate the entire pipeline from prospecting to campaign launch with Apollo's API + Clay enrichment + Instantly sending.
