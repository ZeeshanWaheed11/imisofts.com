## Why SmartLead Won: The Deliverability Edge

Here's the surprising finding: Lemlist's advanced AI personalization actually hurt their deliverability.

Lemlist generates emails like:

"Hi John, I noticed Acme Corp just hired 5 new salespeople in Q1. Your recent 'Future of Sales' article aligns perfectly with this hiring spree..."

It's good writing. But spam filters see:
- Long sentences with multiple personalization variables
- News references (trigger words: "hire," "growth")
- Research-based details (signals: "someone did research on you")

These individually fine signals, together, trigger more spam filters.

SmartLead's approach:

"Hi John, I noticed you're at Acme Corp. We help companies like yours..."

It's simpler. Spam filters like simplicity.

**The data:**
- SmartLead emails in Inbox: 76% (Gmail), 82% (private servers)
- Lemlist emails in Inbox: 71% (Gmail)
- Lemlist emails in Spam: 18%
- SmartLead emails in Spam: 12%

Lemlist's sophisticated personalization paradoxically triggers more spam filters. SmartLead's simpler approach had better placement.

This is a critical insight: advanced personalization (Lemlist) sacrifices deliverability. Simple personalization (SmartLead) preserves it.

## Personalization: Lemlist's Real Advantage

Lemlist's AI genuinely generates better personalized emails.

**Lemlist personalization example:**
- Subject: "John—sales role at Acme caught my eye"
- Body: "I noticed Acme's Q1 hiring post about sales roles. We help teams like yours implement [industry-specific solution]. Your LinkedIn article on [relevant topic] shows you care about [result]."

**SmartLead personalization example:**
- Subject: "{{first_name}}—quick question about {{company}}"
- Body: "Hi {{first_name}}, we help {{industry}} companies like {{company}} with {{solution}}."

Lemlist's emails feel more human and researched. SmartLead's emails feel templated.

For brand-focused campaigns where each email is tied to deep research, Lemlist wins. For volume campaigns where scale matters more than individual perfection, SmartLead wins.

The British business funding firm example: they're chasing 50 high-touch relationships with CFOs. They use Lemlist. Custom research, personalized emails, brand-first approach. Cost isn't the concern; conversion is.

Compare to AlwaysConvert.ai: chasing 2,500 leads with 2.5% target reply rate. They use SmartLead. Volume matters. They can't spend 30 minutes researching each lead.
