# SmartLead Pricing Plans (2026): Complete Cost Guide

SmartLead published their pricing. Which makes them unusual in the cold email space.

Here's what each tier actually costs and whether you should upgrade.

---

## SmartLead's Published Pricing

SmartLead uses per-user pricing (based on number of users/team members).

**Tier 1: Starter**

Cost: $99/month per user
Minimum: 1 user = $99/month

What you get:
- 1 user seat
- Up to 5 campaigns
- 10,000 email sequences included
- Basic email templates
- Reporting dashboard
- Integrations: Gmail, Outlook

Best for: Solo founders testing cold email

---

**Tier 2: Pro**

Cost: $199/month per user
Minimum: 1 user = $199/month

What you get:
- 1 user seat
- Unlimited campaigns
- Unlimited email sequences
- 200+ email templates
- Advanced reporting
- A/B testing
- Team collaboration (basic)
- Integrations: Slack, HubSpot, Zapier, API access

Best for: Growing teams, single user optimization focus

---

**Tier 3: Enterprise**

Cost: $399/month per user
Minimum: 3 users (usually) = $1,197/month

What you get:
- Unlimited users
- White-label options
- Custom integrations
- Priority support (24-hour response)
- Advanced analytics
- Account manager assigned
- Custom training

Best for: Agencies, enterprises, companies with 5+ team members

## What SmartLead's Pricing Does NOT Include

SmartLead handles email sending and automation. It does NOT include:

1. **Lead databases** (Apollo, Hunter, etc.)
   - Cost: $99-500/month separately

2. **Email validation**
   - Cost: $100-300/month separately

3. **Warmup service** (included in SmartLead, actually)
   - Cost: $0 (included)

4. **Domain and inbox setup**
   - Cost: $300-600/month (Gmail Workspace for inboxes)
   - Cost: $12-180/year (domain registration)

5. **Custom integrations** (at lower tiers)
   - Cost: $0-200/month (depends on integration)

## Hidden Costs in SmartLead

**Cost #1: Seat expansion**

Start with 1 user. Need 4 users. Cost goes from $99 to $396 (4 × $99).

Each additional user: +$99-399/month (depending on tier)

In Starter: Adding 1 person = +$99
In Enterprise: Adding 1 person = +$399

---

**Cost #2: Team onboarding**

SmartLead is UI-first (unlike Instantly's API-first).

Onboarding is fast: 2-4 hours per person

But if you have 5 new team members: 10-20 hours of setup time
Cost: 10-20 hours × $75/hour = $750-1,500 in labor

---

**Cost #3: Email sequences and template management**

SmartLead includes templates. But creating custom sequences takes time.

Time per sequence: 2-4 hours
Complex automations: 8-16 hours

If you create 10 sequences/month: 20-40 hours/month = $1,500-3,000/month in labor

## SmartLead Hidden Costs Most People Miss

The advertised pricing is only part of the story. Here are costs that catch teams off guard:

**Email account costs.** SmartLead doesn't include email accounts. You need to bring your own. If you use Google Workspace at $6/user/month, 100 inboxes = $600/month = $7,200/year just for email accounts. With private server infrastructure from imisofts, those same 100 inboxes cost roughly $978/year. The difference is staggering.

**Warmup credits.** SmartLead includes warmup in all plans, but the warmup quality depends on the network size of your plan tier. Basic plans have smaller warmup pools, which means slower warmup and potentially lower inbox placement rates.

**API access.** If you want to integrate SmartLead with your CRM, n8n workflows, or other tools via API, you need at least the Pro plan at $94/month. The Basic plan has limited API access that won't support most automation workflows we build for clients.

**Lead storage limits.** Each plan has a cap on total leads stored. The Basic plan allows 2,000 active leads. For campaigns targeting 5,000-10,000 prospects, you'll need Pro or higher. We've had clients hit this limit within their first month of campaigns.
