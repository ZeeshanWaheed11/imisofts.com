FAQ

Pricing Questions Answered

Everything you need to know about our pricing, billing, and how we work.

Do you require long-term contracts? +

No. All imisofts services are month-to-month. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We believe in earning your business every month, not locking you into contracts. Most clients stay for 6+ months because of results, not obligations.

What is included in the monthly price? +

Each plan includes the full scope of work described, a dedicated specialist or team, weekly reporting and communication, all tools and software needed for execution, and ongoing optimization. There are no hidden fees, setup charges, or surprise costs.

Can I switch between plans or services? +

Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or switch services at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. If you need a custom combination of services, we can create a tailored package during your discovery call.

How quickly can you start? +

Most services begin within 48 hours of signing. Your dedicated specialist is assigned immediately, conducts an initial audit or discovery session, and starts active work in the first week. Full setup and first measurable results typically arrive within 2 to 4 weeks depending on the service.

Do you offer discounts for annual commitments? +

While we do not require annual contracts, clients who commit to 6 or 12 months receive a 10 to 15 percent discount on their monthly rate. This is entirely optional. You can start month-to-month and switch to an annual plan at any time to lock in savings.
