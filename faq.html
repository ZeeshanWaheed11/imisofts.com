Quick answers to common questions about our services and processes

What does imisofts do in one sentence? ▼

We build AI automation, Shopify stores, and product-grade software that delivers measurable ROI in weeks, not months.

Who is a good fit and who is not? ▼

Good fit: Companies with $1M+ revenue looking to automate operations, scale e-commerce, or build AI products. Teams ready to invest in systems that reduce manual work.

Not a good fit: Early-stage startups without product-market fit. Companies expecting miracles without clear goals. Teams unwilling to share data or iterate.

How do we start a project? ▼

Book a 20-minute audit call. We'll discuss your goals, review your current systems, and propose a solution. If we're a fit, you'll get a fixed-price proposal with clear deliverables and timeline.

Do you work outside Dubai and GCC? ▼

Yes. We serve clients globally with teams in Dubai, US, UK, and EU. We adapt to your timezone and preferred communication channels.

Do you offer pilots or proof of value sprints? ▼

Yes. We offer 2-week sprints starting at $4,999 to prove ROI on a specific use case before scaling. Perfect for testing AI automation or new channels.

What tools do you prefer to work in? ▼

We prefer n8n, Next.js, Shopify, HubSpot, and Postgres. We adapt when there's a clear reason. Communication via Slack or WhatsApp, project tracking in Linear or Notion.

How do you price projects? ▼

Fixed-fee packages based on complexity: Starter $4,999-$14,999, Growth $15,000-$29,999, Scale $30,000+. Clear scope, no surprises.

What is included and excluded in your fixed fees? ▼

Included: All development, testing, deployment, documentation, and 30 days of support.

Excluded: Third-party licenses, API costs, hosting fees, and scope changes after approval.

Do you charge for third-party tools and subscriptions? ▼

Third-party costs are passed through at cost with no markup. We'll estimate these upfront and can manage subscriptions on your behalf or transfer ownership.

Payment schedule and accepted methods? ▼

50% upfront, 50% on delivery for projects under $20,000. Larger projects: 40% upfront, 30% at midpoint, 30% on delivery.

We accept wire transfers, credit cards via Stripe, and regional payment methods.

Refunds, change requests, and scope control? ▼

No refunds after work begins. Change requests quoted separately. Scope documented in SOW with approval required for changes. We track all requests in writing.

Do you offer monthly retainers for support? ▼

Yes. Retainers from $2,999/month for 20 hours. Unused hours don't roll over. Priority support with 4-hour response time.

How do you handle access to client systems? ▼

Access is least privilege with audit logs. Secrets are stored in a managed vault. We work inside your tenants whenever possible.

Where is data stored and who owns it? ▼

You own your data, your code, and your accounts. Data remains in your cloud accounts unless agreed otherwise. We never retain client data after project completion.

Do you sign NDAs and DPAs? ▼

Yes. We sign mutual NDAs before discussing sensitive information and DPAs for GDPR compliance. Standard templates available or we can review yours.

How do you treat PII and secrets? ▼

PII is encrypted at rest and in transit. Secrets stored in HashiCorp Vault or cloud KMS. No hardcoded credentials. Regular security audits.

On-premise or hybrid deployment options? ▼

Yes. We can deploy on-premise, private cloud, or hybrid architectures. Additional setup fees apply for complex deployment requirements.

Audit logs and access reviews? ▼

All access logged with timestamps and actions. Monthly access reviews with reports. Immediate revocation upon project completion or team changes.

Typical timelines for Starter, Growth, and Scale packages? ▼

Starter: 2 weeks for MVPs and quick wins.

Growth: 4-6 weeks for production-ready systems.

Scale: 6-10 weeks for enterprise features and integrations.

How you plan sprints and demos? ▼

Weekly sprints with Friday demos. Sprint planning on Monday, daily async updates, demo and feedback on Friday. Everything tracked in Linear or Jira.

What you need from the client to stay on schedule? ▼

Timely feedback within 24 hours. Access to systems and stakeholders. Clear decision-maker for approvals. Sample data and business rules documentation.

How you handle delays or dependencies? ▼

We communicate blockers immediately with mitigation options. Client-side delays may adjust timeline. We work on parallel tracks to minimize idle time.

Handover materials at the end of a project? ▼

Complete documentation, source code, deployment guides, admin credentials, architecture diagrams, and recorded training sessions. Everything in a shared drive.

Training format and who attends? ▼

2-hour recorded sessions for admins and end users. Live Q&A for stakeholders. Documentation site with videos. Follow-up sessions available.

What is included in 30-day post-launch support? ▼

Bug fixes, minor adjustments, performance tuning, and user questions. Response within 4 hours during business hours. Does not include new features or major changes.

Response and resolution targets? ▼

Critical: 2-hour response, same-day resolution. High: 4-hour response, 48-hour resolution. Normal: 24-hour response, 5-day resolution.

How to report an issue and priority levels? ▼

Email growth@imisofts.com or WhatsApp for urgent issues. Include screenshots, steps to reproduce, and impact. We'll assign priority based on business impact.

After-hours or high-season coverage? ▼

Available with premium support plans. 24/7 coverage during critical periods like Black Friday. Surge pricing applies for emergency after-hours work.

Ongoing maintenance options and pricing? ▼

Basic: $2,999/month for 20 hours. Standard: $4,999/month for 40 hours. Premium: $9,999/month for 80 hours with priority support.

Versioning and change management? ▼

Git-based version control with tagged releases. Staging environment for testing. Blue-green deployments with instant rollback. Change logs for every release.

Contracts, MSAs, and SOWs? ▼

Standard MSA covers general terms. SOW details specific project scope, timeline, and costs. Both required before work begins. Templates available for review.

VAT on invoices for UAE clients? ▼

5% VAT applied to UAE invoices. VAT registration number included. Export services may qualify for zero-rating with proper documentation.

IP ownership and license terms? ▼

You own all custom code and content upon final payment. We retain rights to our frameworks and tools with perpetual license granted to you.

GDPR-capable processes and data export? ▼

GDPR-compliant data handling with export and deletion workflows. User consent management built-in. Data portability in standard formats.

Record retention and deletion policies? ▼

Project records retained for 7 years for tax compliance. Client data deleted immediately after project unless retention agreed. Secure deletion with certificates.

Which workflows do you automate first? ▼

Lead capture and instant WhatsApp reply, ticket triage, VAT receipt OCR, COD confirmation. We target high-volume, repetitive tasks with clear ROI.

Do you build with n8n, Make, or custom code? ▼

Pick the lightest tool that meets reliability and cost goals, mix with custom code where needed. n8n for complex workflows, Make for simple, custom for scale.

How do you measure ROI? ▼

Baseline manual time and error rate, then report saved hours, response times, and revenue lift. Dashboard with real-time metrics and monthly reports.

Can you work with legacy portals that have no API? ▼

Use RPA with guardrails and fallbacks, recommend API alternatives when possible. Browser automation as last resort with monitoring and alerts.

Do you only work on Shopify? ▼

Yes, Shopify only for e-commerce. We're experts in one platform rather than mediocre at many. This includes Shopify Plus and headless commerce.

Do you build dropshipping stores and do product research? ▼

Yes, with supplier vetting, margin math, and delivery time controls. We validate products with data before building. Includes competitor analysis.

How do you reduce COD failures and RTO? ▼

WhatsApp order confirmation, address validation, fraud scoring, and courier reputation tracking. COD to prepaid conversion campaigns.

How do you sync with Noon and Amazon? ▼

Real-time inventory sync, order routing, and unified reporting. Central inventory management prevents overselling. API integration where available.

Do you help with App Store SEO and ranking? ▼

Yes. Keyword research, listing optimization, review management, and backlink strategy. Monthly ranking reports and optimization recommendations.

Can you guide us to the Built for Shopify badge? ▼

Yes. We know the requirements and review process. Code quality, performance benchmarks, and security standards. 90% of our apps achieve certification.

Do you support Arabic and English in bots and sites? ▼

Full bilingual support with proper RTL, translated UI, and WhatsApp templates in both languages. Locale detection and seamless switching.

How do you handle 5% VAT and FTA export files? ▼

Automated VAT calculations, invoice generation with TRN, and FTA-compliant export files. Zero-rating for exports with proper documentation.

Can you ingest Property Finder and Bayut leads into CRM? ▼

Yes. API integration for real-time lead capture, deduplication, and routing. WhatsApp automation for instant response. Analytics on lead quality.

Which couriers do you integrate for COD and NDR? ▼

Aramex, Fetchr, Smsa Express, and regional providers. NDR management with automated retry workflows. Real-time tracking and proof of delivery.

Which payment gateways have the best approval rates in UAE? ▼

Checkout.com, Telr, and PayTabs for cards. Network tokens for 10% higher approval. Apple Pay and Samsung Pay for conversion. COD still 60% of orders.

Can you host close to GCC for lower latency? ▼

Yes. AWS Bahrain, Azure UAE, or local hosting. CDN with regional edge locations. Sub-100ms response times for GCC users.
