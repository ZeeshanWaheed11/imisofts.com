Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about imisofts

What does imisofts do? +

imisofts is a full-stack digital execution partner that builds AI automation systems, cold email marketing infrastructure, B2B lead generation campaigns, web applications, e-commerce stores, and CRM solutions. We serve 500+ enterprises across 30 countries from our base in Dubai, UAE.

Where is imisofts located? +

imisofts is headquartered in Dubai, UAE. We operate with a global remote team spanning 15+ countries, serving clients across the Middle East, North America, Europe, and Asia Pacific.

How long has imisofts been in business? +

imisofts was founded in 2019 in Dubai. Since then, we have completed 500+ projects, generated over $50M in client revenue, and built a global team across 15+ countries.

What industries does imisofts serve? +

We serve SaaS companies, e-commerce businesses, real estate firms, healthcare organizations, recruitment agencies, financial services, and B2B companies across 30 countries. Our solutions are industry-agnostic but we specialize in B2B outbound sales and operations automation.
