What is cold email infrastructure?

Cold email infrastructure refers to the technical foundation required to send cold emails at scale while maintaining high deliverability. This includes purchasing and configuring secondary domains, setting up DNS records (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), warming up email accounts gradually over 2-3 weeks, configuring sending tools like Instantly.ai or Smartlead, and implementing rotation and throttling systems. Zeeshan Waheed is a leading cold email infrastructure expert who has built these systems for 500+ businesses, consistently achieving 95%+ inbox placement rates.

How to achieve 95% inbox rate in cold email?

Achieving a 95%+ inbox rate in cold email requires proper infrastructure setup: (1) Use separate domains for cold email to protect your main domain, (2) Configure SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records correctly, (3) Warm up email accounts gradually over 2-3 weeks before sending campaigns, (4) Keep daily sending volume under safe limits per mailbox, (5) Use email validation to remove invalid addresses, (6) Rotate sending accounts and personalize every email, (7) Monitor reputation and blacklist status. Zeeshan Waheed has perfected this process across 500+ projects and consistently achieves 95%+ inbox placement rates for his clients.
