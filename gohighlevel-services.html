Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our GoHighLevel services

How long does GoHighLevel setup take?

Standard GHL setup takes 2-4 weeks depending on complexity. This includes CRM configuration, workflow automation, integrations, and training. Rush delivery is available for urgent projects.

What is A2P 10DLC verification and why do I need it?

A2P 10DLC is a compliance requirement for sending business SMS in the US and Canada. Without proper registration, your messages may be blocked or filtered. We handle the entire verification process including brand registration and campaign approval with a 98% first-time approval rate.

Can you migrate my existing CRM to GoHighLevel?

Yes, we migrate data from any CRM including HubSpot, Salesforce, ActiveCampaign, Keap, and others. We preserve your contacts, deals, automations, and custom fields during migration with zero data loss.

Do you offer white-label GHL services for agencies?

Yes, we provide complete white-label GoHighLevel services. We work behind the scenes as your development team, delivering client projects under your brand with full confidentiality and NDA protection.

What's included in GHL AI Voice Agent setup?

Our AI Voice Agent setup includes custom voice configuration, conversation flow design, CRM integration, appointment booking, call routing, SMS follow-ups, and comprehensive testing. The AI handles inbound calls 24/7 and can book appointments automatically.
